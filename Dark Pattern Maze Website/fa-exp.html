Forced Action

Explanation

To make an account for that website, you actually didn't have to input your email, however the design of the window, and the choice to make the 'skip this' option a very small, unassuming link, might've lead you towards thinking it was required. This is forced action.

But it's just my email...

While giving away your email address may seem unassuming, it is in fact essential data, that you should try to share thoughtfully. Advertisers now use email data to track your habits and behavior across different platforms to generate specifically targeted advertising.

"if you’re wondering why you are continuing to see relevant ads despite the rise of privacy tools that combat digital tracking, it’s largely because you are still sharing your email address."

-Brian X. Chen, Consumer Technology Writer for the NYTimes
