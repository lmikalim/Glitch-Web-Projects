Dark Pattern Maze

Navigating deceptive design on the web

What Are Dark Patterns?

Coined by User Experience Designer Harry Brignull,Dark Patterns are deceptive user interfaces that influence user behavior to achieve specific goals such as increasing sign-ups, sales, or engagement at the expense of user transparency, clarity, or autonomy.

In this experience you'll learn about common dark patterns so you can keep yourself, your data, and your money safe on the web.
